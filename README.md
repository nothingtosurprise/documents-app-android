# ONLYOFFICE Documents for Android 📱

[ONLYOFFICE Documents](https://www.onlyoffice.com/office-for-android?utm_source=github&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign=GitHubAppAndroid) is a free office application that turns your Android device into a mobile productivity powerhouse. 

Crafting important presentations on the go? Reviewing spreadsheets between meetings for that last minute change? 

ONLYOFFICE app has you covered.

### Core capabilities include:
- **Document editor:** Full-featured text editing with formatting options
- **Spreadsheet editor:** Advanced calculations with 450+ formulas 
- **Presentation editor:** Create stunning slideshows with rich media
- **PDF viewer:** View and annotate PDFs seamlessly
- **Form filler:** Complete online forms with ease
- **Media player:** Handle photos and videos alongside your documents

## Key features ⭐

### Feature-packed editor suite

Access professional-grade editing tools that rival desktop applications. Create complex spreadsheets with 450+ formulas, design presentations with animations and transitions, format documents with advanced typography, and collaborate in real-time with team members across the globe.

### PDF handling

Easily view both standard and protected PDF files. Unlock the power of text recognition for scanned documents. View and fill out online forms. Easily navigate from field to field. Save the result as a PDF or DOCX file.

### AI-powered productivity

Boost productivity in documents and spreadsheets with built-in AI. Seamlessly connect models like OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, or Ollama for smarter workflows.

### Familiar interface

The familiar toolbar and layout ensure zero learning curve for users transitioning from traditional office suites. Your muscle memory remains intact while gaining enhanced functionality.

## You get complete control on where your documents live 🌐

### On your device
Access and edit documents stored locally on your Android device. Perfect for offline work when connectivity is limited or when you prefer keeping sensitive files private.

### ONLYOFFICE cloud integration  
Connect to your ONLYOFFICE cloud account for real-time collaboration with team members. Share documents, track changes, and work together seamlessly regardless of location.

### Multiple cloud service support
Link your favorite cloud storage services including Dropbox, Google Drive, Box, and others. Edit documents directly from these platforms without the hassle of constant downloading and uploading.

### Custom cloud solutions
Connect to Nextcloud, ownCloud, or any WebDAV-compatible cloud service. Manage, view, and edit documents directly from the app, maintaining your preferred workflow and security standards.
